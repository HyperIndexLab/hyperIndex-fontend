# **HyperIndex DEX Happy Trading Competition 🎁**

📅 **March 4, 2025 · 7 min read**

## **📢 Event Introduction**

HyperIndex DEX is launching the **Happy Trading Competition** to reward active traders!  
This competition focuses on **HSK trading**, where users can **earn points** by:

- Trading HSK
- Cross-chain transactions
- Team collaborations

💰 **Total Prize Pool: 50,000 HSK!**

---

## **📜 Event Rules**

### **🎯 Points Calculation**

- **1 point** for every **800 USD** worth of HSK traded
- **1 point** for each **cross-chain transaction**
- **Team leaders** receive **an extra 10%** of their team members' points

> **Note:** If funds leave the **Hashkey chain** during the event period, cross-chain points will be uniformly counted as **1 point**.

---

### **📈 Team Points Bonus**

The higher the team's total trading volume, the **more bonus points** team members receive!

| **Team Trading Volume (USD)** | **Points Multiplier** |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------- |
| 100,000 USD                   | 1.1x                  |
| 200,000 USD                   | 1.2x                  |
| 300,000 USD                   | 1.3x                  |
| 500,000 USD                   | 1.4x                  |
| 1,000,000 USD                 | 1.5x                  |

#### **🌰 Example**

Jacky joins Tom's team and trades **8,000 USD** worth of HSK:  
✅ Jacky earns **10 points** (8,000 ÷ 800 = 10)  
✅ Tom earns **1 point** (10% of Jacky's points)  
✅ Tom's team trading volume increases by **8,000 USD**

### **📊 Tiered Trading Volume Rewards**

The remaining **40,000 HSK** will be distributed based on users' **trading volume**!

#### **Reward Distribution**

All **eligible participants** will be grouped into different tiers based on their **total effective trading volume** and share the respective **prize pool proportionally**.

| **Trading Volume (USD)** | **Reward Pool (HSK)** | **Distribution Rule**      |
| ------------------------ | --------------------- | -------------------------- |
| 50,000 - 99,999          | 5,000 HSK             | Distributed proportionally |
| 100,000 - 199,999        | 8,000 HSK             | Distributed proportionally |
| 200,000 - 299,999        | 10,000 HSK            | Distributed proportionally |
| 300,000 - 499,999        | 8,000 HSK             | Distributed proportionally |
| 500,000+                 | 9,000 HSK             | Distributed proportionally |

#### **🌰 Example**

- **Alice** trades **120,000 USD** → She qualifies for the **100,000 - 199,999 USD** tier and shares the **8,000 HSK** reward pool with other traders.
- **Bob** trades **80,000 USD** → He qualifies for the **50,000 - 99,999 USD** tier and shares the **5,000 HSK** reward pool.

### **⚠️ Important Rules**

- **Trading volume** is calculated in **USDT equivalent** and applies only to **HSK trading pairs**.
- Each user can only **qualify for the highest tier they reach**.
- Rewards will be **distributed after the event ends**.
